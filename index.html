Momofuku Ando

1910-2007

Momofuku Ando was the inventor of the ultimate food staple of young adults everywhere, instant ramen.

  • 1910 - Born in Gô Peh-hok, Japanese Taiwan, Empire of Japan (now Puzi, Chiayi County, Taiwan)
  • 1932 - Started his own textiles company
  • 1948 - Jailed and went bankrupt
  • 1958 - Marketed the first package of precooked instant noodles
  • 1971 - Invented Cup Noodles, complete with Styrofoam cup
  • 1982 - Received the Order of the Sacred Treasure honor
  • 2002 - Received the Order of the Rising Sun honor
  • 2007 - Died of heart failure

Ando was a great example of how to bounce back from a devastating failure to achieve world-renowned success that continued after his death. Ando also was proof that people can live a long life while eating ramen almost every day.

To learn more about Ando's interesting life, please check out his Wikipedia page.
